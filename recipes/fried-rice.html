Island-Style Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 (12‑ounce) can fully cooked luncheon meat (such as Spam), cubed
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ½ tablespoon Sriracha (optional)
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon shredded nori (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon sliced scallions (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium‑high heat; add Spam. Cook until lightly browned, about 5 to 7 minutes; transfer to a plate.
  2. Heat sesame oil in the same skillet until shimmering (~1 minute). Add onion; sauté until slightly softened (~5 minutes). Reduce heat; add rice and frozen vegetables; cook & stir until heated through (~5 minutes). Push rice mixture to edges of skillet; add the 4 beaten eggs to center; cook & stir until curds form (~3‑4 minutes).
  3. Stir ½ the Spam into the scrambled eggs, then stir into rice mixture; add soy sauce and Sriracha; stir, then remove from heat.
  4. Crack remaining eggs into a separate skillet over medium heat. Cook until outer edges become opaque (~1 minute); cover, reduce heat to low and cook until whites are completely set (~4 minutes); season with salt & pepper.
  5. Place fried eggs on top of fried rice; add remaining ½ the Spam. Garnish with nori, scallions and sesame seeds.
