Spicy Ahi Poke Recipe

Home

Description

This recipe is a local staple in the Kingdom of Hawai'i. Although this dish can me purchased pre-made in the seafood section of many local grocery stores, Here you will be able to learn how to make and enjoy this dish from the comfort of your home.

Ingredients

    Raw Tuna, Shoyu, Sriracha, Sesame Oil, Kewpie Mayo (or regular mayo), Green Onion, Sesame Seeds, Macadamia nuts(optional)

Steps

    Cut the raw tuna into cubed/bite-sized pieces, Finely chop some Green Onion, (optional) Finely chop some Macadamia Nuts, In a bowl add
      1/4 cup Shoyu, 1 tbsp Sriracha, 1 tbsp Sesame Oil, Chopped Green Onion, 1 tsp Sesame Seeds, chopped macadamia nuts
    Mix thoroughly, Cover with plastic wrap and store in the fridge to chill until time to eat., Plate and enjoy!
