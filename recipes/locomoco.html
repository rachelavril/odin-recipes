Hawaiian Loco Moco

Ingredients

  • 2 (4‑ounce) patties ground beef
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1½ cups beef stock
  • 4 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 2 teaspoons ketchup
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar (optional)
  • 2 drops sesame oil (or to taste)
  • 2 teaspoons unsalted butter, divided
  • 2 tablespoons minced green onions (white & light green parts)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups hot cooked white rice

Instructions

  1. Season patties with salt, pepper, and cayenne; set aside.
  2. Whisk together beef stock, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, cornstarch, ketchup, sugar, and sesame oil; set aside.
  3. Melt 1 teaspoon butter in a skillet over medium‑high heat. Cook patties until bottoms are browned (~5 minutes). Flip; add green onions and cook until beef and onions are browned (~4‑5 minutes). Remove patties and most green onions; place on a plate.
  4. Pour beef stock mixture into skillet. Stir, scraping up browned bits, until thickened (~2 minutes). Reduce heat, reserve gravy.
  5. Melt remaining butter in another skillet. Fry eggs side‑by‑side until whites are set and yolks remain runny.
  6. Divide rice between 2 bowls. Place patties over rice, spoon gravy on top, add fried eggs.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
